Triple Berry Crisp

Description

This Triple Berry Crisp with fresh strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries has a delicious cinnamon-oat topping, is excellent with any combination of berries, and pairs well with this homemade whipped cream.

Ingredients

For the Filling

  • 1 ½ cups sliced strawberries
  • 1 ½ cups blueberries
  • 1 ½ cups raspberries
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • ¼ cup sugar

For the Topping

  • 1 cup quick oats
  • 3 tbsp light brown sugar, not packed
  • ¾ tsp cinnamon
  • 2 ½ tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Heat over to 375F degrees.
  2. Combine strawberries, blueberries, respberries, sugar, ¼ tsp cinnamon, and lemon zest in large bowl.
  3. Sprinkle with cornstarch. Toss until fruit is coated.
  4. Place fruit in an ungreased pie dish.
  5. Mix remaining topping ingredients in a medium bowl and sprinkle over fruit.
  6. Bake about 40 minutes or until topping is golden brown and fruit is tender.
